    • Grookey Pokedex

    Grookey is a Grass type Pokémon introduced in Generation 8. It is known as the Chimp Pokémon. Grookey is one of the starter Pokémon available in Pokémon Sword & Shield (releasing late 2019) along with Scorbunny and Sobble. It is a mischievous Pokémon that is full of boundless curiosity. During the Sword & Shield announcement trailer it is seen tapping the stick it keeps in its hair, then climbing to the top of a building. Grookey appears to be based on a spider monkey.
