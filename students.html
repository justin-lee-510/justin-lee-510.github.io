Meet the Studio

Meet my piano studio! My private lessons consist of learning new repetoire, technique, music theory, ear training, stage presence, and more. These many aspects of music help my students become well-rounded musicians.
Below is a short description of each student with a video of their favorite performance! These star students all work very hard and practice many times a week.

Leland

Meet Leland! Leland was my very first student. I started teaching him when he was eight years old. Now, he's entering 6th grade! Leland is a very smart and hard worker. In his free time, Leland enjoys taking care of his dog Cheyenne and playing video games. When he practices, he always has an audience of stuffed animals who enjoy his music.

Kenneth

Meet Kenneth! Kenneth is the newest student in the studio. He started piano this March, and although he hasn't had an in-person lesson with me yet, he is playing through his pieces with flying colors! Thanks to his hard work and supportive parents, Kenneth is already growing to be a strong musician. He will be entering kindergarten, although he sounds like a mature third grader! Besides piano, Kenneth enjoys spending time with his sister Cheryl, playing with cars, and building Legos.
In this video, Kenneth is performing three pieces: Forest Drums, Lightly Row, and Sleeping Beauty Waltz.

Allie

Meet Allie! Allie is currently in 5th grade. Allie and Aria are sisters! Allie is also a quick learner, and is great at listening and keeping a good attitude in class. Besides practicing piano, Allie spends her free time playing with her sister, reading, and also taking care of her dog. Fun fact: Both Allie and Aria's aunt is famous Masterchef Winner Christine Ha, who won in the show's third season.

Lukas

Meet Lukas! Lukas is in the 7th grade. He has been learning piano with me for two years. He is a quick learner and has a great grasp on rhythm and ear training. Besides piano, Lukas is also talented in cooking and riding the ripstick. He also enjoys playing with his friends and eating yummy food.

Cheryl

Meet Cheryl! Cheryl is a sixth grade musician. She is an excellent listener and extremely hard worker. Every lesson, she comes prepared to show me her weekly accomplishments. Over the summer, she has been able to progress very quickly through the material. She is a multi-talented musician as well: well versed in the drum set and guitar! Outside of piano, Cheryl enjoys reading novels, drawing, uploading videos on her YouTube channel, and playing on the computer. In this video, she is performing Diabelli: Sonatina Op. 168 No. 3 in C Major. You can check out her YouTube channel here!
